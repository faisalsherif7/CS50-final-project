how it works

Any income that you add will be recorded in a table and tracked until one Hijri year has passed. After that, the corresponding zakat amount will be added to the 'Zakat Due' table, which you can check at any time. The expectation is that zakat for that amount will be paid, but if it is not paid on time, it will remain in the Zakat Due table until you indicate that it has been paid. Assuming the zakat for a given year is paid, the remaining savings will be used to calculate the new zakat amount for the following year, and so on.

If you ensure that your expenses are entered accurately, we will deduct them in reverse chronological order starting from the most recent income and working backwards through previous incomes.
